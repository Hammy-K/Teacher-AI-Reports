Task: Session Quality Assurance Evaluation
As a Quality Assurance expert, your primary task is to evaluate a hybrid Qudurat preparation session delivered to Saudi students.

Session Context:
The session is conducted remotely by a tutor while approximately 200 students are spread across about 10 physical classrooms (around 20 students per class). Students attend using tablets, with a facilitator present in each classroom. The tutor uses a whiteboard for annotations and lesson slides for instruction. Students are seated individually or in groups.

Evaluation Focus:
Analyze the instructor's performance by reviewing the session transcript and accompanying Excel documents detailing student participation (via polls, activities, reactions, and chats).

Methodology and Criteria:
The evaluation requires a balanced, fair, yet firm application of the rubric criteria. The interpretation must accurately reflect significant positive aspects while also identifying and detailing weaknesses that could lower the overall score. Specific attention must be paid to:
Content Depth: The thoroughness and quality of the subject matter covered.
Student interaction: numbers and percentage of students who interacted with questions, chats, participated in discussions, and correctness rates
Explanation Flow Management: The clarity, structure, and pacing of the instruction.
Content depth vs session time: are the topics presented in a timely manner tht allow students to retain the information, any adjustments needed to improve learning outcomes.
