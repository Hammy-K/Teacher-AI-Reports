ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENT — Teacher Communication & Motivational Style Analysis

Extend transcript analysis to evaluate teacher communication effectiveness, tone, and motivational behavior. The system must assess how the teacher explains concepts, interacts emotionally with students, and uses reinforcement techniques.

All insights must be evidence-based and derived from transcript and chat data.

4.Y Teacher Explanation Effectiveness Evaluation

The system must evaluate whether the teacher explained concepts effectively and provide coaching-style feedback.

Step 1 — Identify Explanation Segments

Detect transcript segments where the teacher is explaining content. These include:

Concept definitions

Problem-solving walkthroughs

Instruction before activities

Clarification after student mistakes

Merge consecutive transcript lines with ≤5s gap into explanation blocks.

Step 2 — Evaluate Explanation Quality

For each explanation block, analyze:

Structure Quality

Detect if explanation includes:

Clear concept introduction

Logical step-by-step breakdown

Example or demonstration

Summary or reinforcement statement

Comprehension Support

Detect if teacher:

Asked verification questions

Encouraged students to respond

Checked student understanding

Provided alternative explanation phrasing

Step 3 — Generate Explanation Coaching Feedback

Output:

Timestamp:
{Time range}

Concept:
{Detected topic}

What Teacher Did Well:
Narrative highlighting strengths such as:

Clarity

Structure

Engagement

Use of examples

Student involvement

How Explanation Could Be Improved:
Specific coaching suggestions such as:

Simplifying language

Adding verification questions

Breaking explanation into shorter segments

Using real-world analogies

Increasing student interaction

Evidence:
Arabic transcript excerpt

Impact Prediction:
Narrative describing how explanation quality may influence student understanding and correctness results.

4.Y.2 Positive Reinforcement & Motivation Analysis

Detect whether teacher reinforces correct answers and motivates student participation.

Detect Reinforcement Behaviors

Identify if teacher:

Praised correct answers

Acknowledged student effort

Celebrated class performance

Encouraged retry after mistakes

Motivated students before activities

Used inclusive language (e.g., "let’s solve together")
